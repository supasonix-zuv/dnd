A couple corrections
merwini 19 Oct 2021 18:03

Creation's casting time is incorrectly set as 1 action. Should be 1 minute.
Tenser's Transformation is correctly 1 action in the body of the page, but in the tags is "Special" instead.

Also several minor typos I've noticed (bolded for emphasis):
On the front page, the Dragon Wings racial feat is spelled "Dragon Winga", although it's correct on its actual page.
The Components section for Earthquake says "a pinch o f dirt, a piece o f rock"
Dark Star description includes two typos right next to each other "A creature with darkvision can't set though the magical darkness", should be see through
Illusory Dragon has a random apostrophe in the description "taking '7d6 damage"
Vedalken race has a space in the middle of a word "Whenever you make an ability check with the chosen s kill or tool"

Maddening Darkness used em dashes in the description instead of hyphens which isn't really a typo but just looks weird. "60—foot—radius"
