cottoneyenoh 06 Mar 2021 19:03

I think that the Revenant works better as its own page under our UA section than as being listed as a subrace for the few examples listed in the original UA it came from. The article mentions how there's no subraces for half-elves and half-orcs (which we know is wrong) and doesn't mention the other races that it could theoretically apply to (elves, dwarves, and the variety of races that were released after the article was published), so keeping it as its own standalone page works better imo.

Our Backgrounds section could certainly use some adjustment, separating the generic backgrounds (fisher, hermit, etc) from the setting specific backgrounds (the Ravnica guilds and most of the backgrounds that still need to be added). I think that formatting it to match our Races section might work, but that's a decision for Fredbob or the admin to make.
