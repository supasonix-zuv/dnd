Unlinked Articles and Page Consolidation
Out of Time 24 Nov 2020 09:57

It has come to my attention that this wiki has some unlinked pages, some of which have a lot of text, like the pages for the plane shift planes, which are sources for some major things like subclasses, sometimes spells, and feats. They are currently only accessible through specific searches of the wiki's search bar. It would be convenient for there to be a page organizing each link in the wiki into groups, like the main page, but more expansive.

Also, directory with pages for each of the major sourcebooks and the things stemming from them would be useful to people who can use only a few sources, like people participating in adventurer's league. Also, links from each page in a sourcebook linking back to their sourcebook page would tie everything neatly together.
