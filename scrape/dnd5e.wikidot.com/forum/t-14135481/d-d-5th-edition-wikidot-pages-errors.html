[D&D 5th Edition] wikidot pages errors
Oracle-Raven 03 Jul 2021 04:38

Regarding the wikidot pages for D&D 5th edition spells, I noticed a few errors.

1. The pages which list the "Alarm" spell and the "Purify Food and Drink" spell are missing the Ritual tag on the list of all spells, under the "1st Level" tab.

2. Although the page for the "Alarm" spell does mention it is a "Ritual", it still does not appear on the list of all Ritual spells.

3. The page for the "Purify Food and Drink" spell does not mention it is a ritual, even though it is.

4. The "Purify Food and Drink" spell also does not appear on the list of all Ritual spells.

Re: [D&D 5th Edition] wikidot pages errors
eruhcvnxb 03 Jul 2021 09:25

I'm looking through the druid spells and there's a bunch of ones listed as part of the druid spell list, that do not belong there. If a spell is granted by an archetype, it is not in general part of the spell list, so it should not have the Spell list:druid at the bottom, nor appear on the spell:druid page.

This concerns eg. Divination, Aura of Vitality, Revivify, Augury, Cone of Cold

Aura of Vitality is also not part of the Cleric Spell list, revivify not a ranger spell, augury not a wizard spell,
