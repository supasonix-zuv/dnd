Spell Points: http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell-points

Note, I made a personal homebrew change to it, clearly marked by it being Italicized, as well as having subscript reading Homebrew, to make a modification so as that artificers can use the spell point system. Should this be a thing, even if clearly noted? Since, it is not in the original version of the feature, however the feature does not account for the existance of artificers which were created after. Additionally, while you could just use the rules for the other half casters, this would not be perfect, as artificers round half their level up, rather than down, to determine spell points.
