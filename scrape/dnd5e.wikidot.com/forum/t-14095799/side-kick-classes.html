Side Kick Classes
NCat 12 May 2021 01:43

So, this website, in my eyes atleast, is a large collection of all the dnd content available to players. This brings me to my question, should we add the side kick classes?

For those who dont know, the side kick classes were a set of options that were released in Tashas, which were a set of 3 NPC classes, intended to fill the role of giving a non player character level progression and features, without the complexity of classes and subclasses.

Personally, I feel like they should belong on the wiki, due to them being class options. However, they should be within their own category, probably towards the bottom of the main page. What are your thoughts?
